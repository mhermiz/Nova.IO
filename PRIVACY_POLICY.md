# Nova.IO Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Nova.IO ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Nova.IO mobile application handles information when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

Nova.IO is designed to work primarily on your device.

At this time, Nova.IO does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information through the app itself.

## Videos and Media Files

When you choose to import a video, Nova.IO accesses the video files that you select from your device so the app can:

- preview your selected video
- generate thumbnails
- apply edits and enhancements
- export edited videos to a location you choose

Your selected videos are processed locally on your device. Nova.IO does not upload your videos to our servers.

## Device Permissions

Nova.IO may request access to photos, videos, media files, or storage on your device solely to support core app functionality such as:

- selecting videos to import
- reading chosen video files
- saving exported videos

These permissions are only used for the features described above.

## Analytics and Tracking

At this time, Nova.IO does not use in-app advertising and does not intentionally use third-party analytics or tracking tools to profile users inside the app.

If analytics, crash reporting, cloud processing, or advertising features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Data Sharing

Nova.IO does not sell your personal information.

Because video processing currently occurs locally on your device, Nova.IO does not share your imported videos with us or with third parties through the app itself.

## Third-Party Services

Nova.IO may be distributed through third-party platforms such as Google Play. Those platforms may collect certain technical or transactional information under their own privacy policies. Please review the applicable platform policies for more information.

## Data Retention

Nova.IO does not maintain a user account database for app users at this time.

Imported and exported video files remain on your device unless you delete them. You are responsible for managing files stored on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Nova.IO is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to design Nova.IO so that video processing occurs locally where possible. However, no method of electronic storage or software operation is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
